Professional Projects

As part of validating my second year of a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia and Internet Professions (BUT MMI), I completed an eight-week internship from May 12 to July 4 at Leclerc Voyages agency located in the Rivetoile shopping center in Strasbourg. This experience continued my professional project and allowed me to apply the skills acquired through my training while discovering how a professional structure operates in the tourism and communication field.

Print Production

Business Cards

During my internship at E.Leclerc Voyages, I modernized the agents' business cards to strengthen brand image and integrate new features (social networks, QR codes). Using Adobe Illustrator and Canva, I designed an attractive and structured visual, combining evocative pictograms and QR codes to Facebook and Instagram, while respecting the graphic charter. The main challenge was balancing readability and aesthetics in a small format. The result is a clear and modern card, now in use, which facilitates customer contact and reflects the agency's values of proximity and reliability. This project allowed me to apply my graphic design skills, respond to a concrete need, and learn to work with real constraints.

Google and Facebook Review Flyers

During my internship at Leclerc Voyages Strasbourg Rivétoile agency, I redesigned two flyers to encourage customers to follow the agency on Facebook and leave a Google review. The objective was to strengthen digital visibility and the connection between physical and digital media. Created with Canva and Illustrator, the new visuals, clearer and more coherent, include pictograms and QR codes. This project allowed me to apply my graphic design skills and contribute to the agency's social media engagement.

Gift Card

During my internship at Leclerc Voyages Strasbourg Rivétoile agency, I redesigned the gift card to modernize it and strengthen its visual impact. Inspired by an airline ticket, the new version, created with Canva and Illustrator, offers structured and evocative layout, respecting the graphic charter. This project allowed me to develop my design and visual communication skills in a real professional context.
