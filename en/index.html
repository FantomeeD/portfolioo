About me

Currently in my 3rd year pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Multimedia and Internet Professions (BUT MMI) at Haguenau, I am focusing on developing my skills in video technique, sound design, post-production, motion design, and graphic design.

By nature creative and energetic, I create videos and draw in my free time. My passions include video games, programming for its fascinating logic, and cinema, particularly the Star Wars saga and its impressive practical and digital effects.

From 2020 to 2023, I studied at a high school in the United States where I developed my English to C1 level. During these 3 years of studies, I participated in the school newspaper through journalism courses. I explored video game creation from A to Z in Virtual Reality with Unity, drawing, and 3D creation of machining parts with AutoCAD. Thus, I developed many skills allowing me to excel in my passions and produce personal and professional projects.
